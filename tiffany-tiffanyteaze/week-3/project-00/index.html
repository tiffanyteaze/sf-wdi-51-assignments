Tiffany Teaze

I'm a frank and eclectic web developer

Hi, I'm Tiffany.

My passion for web development started when I was a teenager building fan sites for my favorite bands, and I continued my curiosity for coding in college and my adult life. The past eight years I’ve spent helping customers troubleshoot their technical products have helped me develop the problem solving skills required for building applications.

Front-End Development

I attended the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly in San Francisco for twelve weeks to meet user needs, model data, develop wireframes, and work collaboratively through version control. I built applications from the ground up to create dynamic front-ends with Javascript, Jinja2 and React as well as back-end environments with Node, Express, Flask, Postgres and Mongoose, and incorporated third party APIs with Axios and AJAX calls.
